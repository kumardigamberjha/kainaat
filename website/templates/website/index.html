Kainaat Mahajan

Kainaat is a 1-year old baby who belongs to a Punjabi family living in Bangalore. Her father works in a foreign bank and mom manages her, the house and her content writing projects from home.

Kainaat is a happy-go-lucky baby who loves to spread joy wherever she goes. She makes friends very easily and has some great friends across the borders at this young age.

You can find her routine shenanigans, travel adventures, love for dogs and ice creams on her instagram handle @tanynkai

This website is a tiny space dedicated to this wonder baby where we intend to chronicle Kainaat’s journey, the significant moments of her life as well as maintain a blog of Tanya’s motherhood journey with Kainaat. We hope you enjoy spending time here and would love for you to leave a word or two of encouragement in our Feedback section.


                    Love & Light,
                    Raman-Tanya-Kainaat
